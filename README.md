## üîç Key Insights
- The dataset contains multiple job postings across different locations, companies, and sources such as LinkedIn, Indeed, and Glassdoor.
- A small number of companies contribute a large share of total job postings, indicating concentrated hiring.
- Remote opportunities are more common in Data, AI, and Machine Learning roles compared to traditional software roles.
- Yearly salaried jobs have a significantly higher standardized salary compared to hourly roles.
- Recently posted jobs (last 7 days) tend to offer competitive salaries, indicating active market demand.
- AI, Data Analyst, and ML Engineer roles are highly concentrated in major tech locations and remote setups.
- Commute time analysis shows that remote roles eliminate long commute categories, improving job accessibility.
- Salary categorization highlights that a large portion of tech roles fall into mid to high salary brackets.
